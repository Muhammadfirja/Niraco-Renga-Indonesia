🌴 Who We Are

Niraco Renga Indonesia is a manufacturer and exporter of natural arenga & coconut sugar from North Sumatra, Indonesia. We are committed to providing healthy, natural, and responsibly processed food products in partnership with local farmer communities.

Healthy & Natural Products

Arenga & coconut sugar processed naturally without harmful chemicals

Eco-Friendly

Sustainable production processes that preserve nature

Empowering Farmers

Partnering directly with local farmers for mutual prosperity

Premium Quality

Strict quality control to ensure the best products

Our Vision & Mission

We believe the goodness of Indonesian nature deserves to be brought to the global market—through products that are honest, sustainable, and positively impact farmers, consumers, and the environment.

🎯 Vision

To be a leading coconut sugar producer connecting Indonesian local wisdom with global standards.

🚀 Mission

To deliver high-quality coconut sugar products while empowering local farmers and preserving nature.
